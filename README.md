## What are the 9 Rasas?

| Rasa | Sanskrit | Emotion |
|------|----------|---------|
| Shringara | शृङ्गार | Love & Beauty |
| Hasya | हास्य | Joy & Laughter |
| Karuna | करुण | Compassion & Sorrow |
| Raudra | रौद्र | Fury & Passion |
| Vira | वीर | Heroism & Courage |
| Bhayanaka | भयानक | Fear & Dread |
| Bibhatsa | बीभत्स | Disgust & Revulsion |
| Adbhuta | अद्भुत | Wonder & Awe |
| Shanta | शान्त | Peace & Serenity |

## How It Works

1. Paste any text content
2. The engine scores it across all 9 Rasas using Claude AI + Vedic keyword mapping
3. Returns dominant Rasa, full emotional spectrum, content insight, and virality score

**Framework:** Sutra XIII of the Divine Earthly Vedic Sutras — Natya Shastra / Rasa Sutras mapped to Sentiment Analysis & Affective Computing.
